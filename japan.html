Japan — Wonders to Discover

Experience Japan’s rich culture, natural beauty, and architectural wonders. Here are three must-see landmarks with nearby hotel options.

Mount Fuji

Japan’s tallest peak and a UNESCO World Heritage site, Mount Fuji offers breathtaking scenery and is a sacred cultural symbol. Hiking trails and serene lakes nearby make it a dream destination for nature lovers.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Located in Kyoto, this Shinto shrine is famous for its thousands of vibrant red torii gates that form mesmerizing paths up the mountain. It’s a spiritual site that blends history, culture, and natural beauty.
